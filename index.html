About the Project

Learning by Doing in the Heart of Thailand

As part of our MBA program, we were given the opportunity to work directly with a real company on a real challenge. We chose Hilltribe Organics — a Northern Thailand social enterprise that has spent over a decade improving the lives of hill tribe farming families through sustainable organic agriculture.

Our mission: understand the business deeply, spend time with the farmers and the team in the field, and deliver strategic recommendations that can genuinely move the needle.

Over 13 days, we moved from Bangkok to Chiang Rai, into the farms of Wawee Valley, and back through Chiang Mai — experiencing both the country and the company from the inside.

Our Client

Hilltribe Organics

A Northern Thailand social enterprise over a decade in the making — and a masterclass in what business can look like when profit and purpose pull in the same direction.

Hilltribe Organics was founded with a simple but powerful belief: that with the right support, sustainable contract organic farming can produce exceptional products and be genuinely attractive to farming families. Based in the hills of Northern Thailand, the company works directly with hill tribe farming communities, teaching organic agriculture and introducing high-value crops as a sustainable alternative to conventional — and sometimes harmful — farming practices.

Their flagship product — 100% organic, free-range eggs — became the first certified organic eggs produced in Thailand when they launched in 2013. Today, Hilltribe Organics exports internationally and their products are available at major Thai retailers including Tops, Makro, Lotus, Villa Market, and Gourmet Market.

  • 🌱 Teaches organic farming to hill tribe communities
  • 🏡 Provides stable income so families can stay in their villages
  • 🚫 Eliminates chemical pesticides and harmful farming practices
  • 🐓 Hens live 3+ years across 2+ acres of land per farm
  • 🌍 Products now exported internationally
